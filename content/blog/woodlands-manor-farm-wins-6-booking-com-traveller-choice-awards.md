We’re pleased to share the exciting news that **six of our seven holiday cottages at Woodlands Manor Farm have just been awarded the prestigious [Travellers’ Choice Award!](https://www.booking.com/traveller-review-awards/index.en-gb.html)** This is a huge honour and a moment of pride for our whole team.

From [_Rose Cottage_,](https://woodlandsmanorfarm.co.uk/rose-cottage/) which received an unbeatable 10/10, to [_The Coach House_,](https://woodlandsmanorfarm.co.uk/the-coach-house/) [_Jasmine_](https://woodlandsmanorfarm.co.uk/jasmine-cottage/), [_Honeysuckle_](https://woodlandsmanorfarm.co.uk/honeysuckle-cottage/), _[The Manor House, Holiday Cottage with Pool, Bude](https://woodlandsmanorfarm.co.uk/the-manor-house/)_ and [_Lavender_](https://woodlandsmanorfarm.co.uk/lavender-cottage/) – each of these beautiful cottages received the award thanks to outstanding guest reviews and consistently high star ratings. – each of these special places has received incredible recognition, and it’s all thanks to **you, our wonderful guests**. The only cottage that missed out was _The Stables. And thats_ only because it’s so popular that guests usually book direct with us, meaning it rarely appears on third-party platforms like Booking.com. That’s a good problem to have!

We want to take a heartfelt moment to thank each and every one of you who has taken the time to write a review after your stay. At a time when independent businesses like ours face ever-increasing costs and challenges, your kind words, honest feedback, and five-star ratings truly mean the world to us.

We are also deeply grateful to our booking partners, including Airbnb and Booking.com, for helping to connect us with so many wonderful guests and running marekting such as the Travellers Choice Awards.. Your support continues to play a big role in our success.

Every glowing review is not just a pat on the back – it’s a boost that keeps us motivated and inspired to keep delivering the very best holiday experience we can.

## **Why Reviews Matter More Than Ever**

In today’s digital world, reviews are more important than ever. They help guests feel confident when booking, they guide us in fine-tuning everything we offer, and they directly contribute to awards like these.

We are incredibly grateful that so many of you continue to take the time to leave thoughtful feedback. Whether it’s a few kind words about how the kids loved the feed-the-animals experience, praise for the peaceful setting, the thoughtful welcome hamper, or notes on how we can improve, it’s all so valuable. Your reviews help keep our little business thriving.
