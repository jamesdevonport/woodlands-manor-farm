### Recommended Fishing Spots

_Summerleaze Beach – A Popular Spot with Anglers_

**Bude Canal**: The [Bude Canal](https://www.budecanalangling.co.uk/) offers excellent freshwater fishing opportunities. Known for its abundance of bream, roach, and carp, the canal provides a tranquil setting for a peaceful day of fishing. With its picturesque surroundings and calm waters, the Bude Canal is a must-visit spot for anglers.

**Tamar Lakes**: Just a short drive from Woodlands Manor Farm, Tamar Lakes is a haven for fishing enthusiasts. Consisting of two stunning lakes, [Upper Tamar](https://www.swlakestrust.org.uk/upper-tamar) and [Lower Tamar](https://www.swlakestrust.org.uk/lower-tamar), this location offers a tranquil setting surrounded by beautiful countryside. Here, you can catch a variety of fish species including carp, pike, perch, and tench.

**Summerleaze Beach**: If you prefer sea fishing, [Summerleaze Beach](https://www.visitbude.info/for-beach-lovers/beaches/summerleaze-beach/) is a fantastic choice. With its sandy shores and rocky outcrops, this beach offers diverse fishing options. Cast your line and try your luck at catching mackerel, bass, and even sea trout. The rugged beauty of Summerleaze Beach combined with the thrill of sea fishing makes it an unforgettable experience.

**Widemouth Bay**: For those keen on coastal fishing, [Widemouth Bay](https://www.visitbude.info/for-beach-lovers/beaches/widemouth-bay/) is a popular choice. This beach provides an excellent opportunity to catch a variety of sea fish including thornback rays, dogfish, and bass. It’s a fantastic option for a day out by the ocean, casting your line into the Atlantic waves.
