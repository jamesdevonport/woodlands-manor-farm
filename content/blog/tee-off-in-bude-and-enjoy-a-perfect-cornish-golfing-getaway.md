### Enjoy Our Top 5 Diverse Golf Courses in and Around Bude

_Bude and North Cornwall Golf Club_

During your stay at Woodlands Manor Farm you are just a short drive from golf courses that caters to all skill levels and preferences. Here are a few highlights:

**[Bude and North Cornwall Golf Club](http://www.budegolf.co.uk/ "Bude and North Cornwall Golf Club")**

Located in the heart of Bude, this course is a classic links course with many blind shots and fast undulating greens that is challenging and enjoyable for golfers of all levels. With natural and well-maintained fairways and greens complemented by breathtaking sea views, the course at Bude and North Cornwall Golf Club offers an unforgettable 18 holes.

[**Holsworthy Golf Club**](https://www.holsworthygolfclub.co.uk/ "Holsworthy Golf Club")

Surrounded by the rolling hills of the Devon countryside, Holsworthy Golf Club is an 18-hole, par 72 course that offers a peaceful and enjoyable golfing experience. Challenging but fair for golfers of all levels, it is situated in the heart of Ruby Country and offers stunning scenery, including views of the National Park of Dartmoor from the 10th tee.

[**Ivyleaf Golf Course**](https://www.ivyleafgolf.com/ "Ivyleaf Golf Course")

This 9-hole, 2300-yard golf course is known for its beautiful views of the Cornish coastline and its relaxed atmosphere. There is no dress code or tee time reservations required, making it a great option for beginners or those looking for a casual round of golf. The course also offers a driving range, golf simulator, and other amenities.

[**Trethorne Golf Club**](https://trethornegolfclub.com/ "Trethorne Golf Club")

One of the most popular golf courses in the region, Trethorne Golf Course is a beautiful 18-hole golf course featuring a challenging and enjoyable layout set in a picturesque landscape. Having received significant investment over the past five years, the course offers a golfing experience that is second to none.

[**Royal North Devon Golf Club**](https://www.royalnorthdevongolfclub.co.uk/ "Royal North Devon Golf Club")

Often referred to as the _“St Andrews of the South”_ due to its historical significance, Royal North Devon is the oldest golf course in England and definitely not one to be missed. Designed by the legendary Old Tom Morris, on this 18-hole course you’ll have a day on the greens like no other as you enjoy free-flowing golf amongst the sheep and horses who call the course home.

### Golf for the Whole Family

_Bude Haven Recreation Ground Crazy Golf_

If you’re looking for a golf experience that is a little different than a traditional golf course, Bude also has you covered:

[**Pitch & Putt**](https://www.bude-stratton.gov.uk/your-parks-green-spaces/pitch-putt/ "Pitch & Putt")

During the summer months you can head to the Pitch & Putt at Summerleaze Downs to enjoy a casual afternoon out. Whether you’re with friends and family, enjoying a solo outing, or introducing youngsters to the sport, pitch & putt provides an enjoyable escape that is suitable for the whole family with the satisfying reward of sinking that putt.

**[Crazy Golf](https://budehavenrec.com/ "Crazy Golf")**

Traditional golfing skills go out of the window when it comes to crazy golf! With wacky obstacles and unique challenges to overcome, each hole offers a different puzzle to navigate. Whether you’re a seasoned golfer or a complete beginner, crazy golf levels the playing field and offers a fun experience for players of all ages.
