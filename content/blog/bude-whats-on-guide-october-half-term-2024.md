**Why Bude is Perfect destination for** **October Half Term**

Bude, a picturesque coastal town in North Cornwall, is famous for its rugged coastline, outdoor activities, stunning beaches, and charming countryside. ## **Family-Friendly Activities in Bude**

### **Bude Beach Adventures**

![Crooklets Beach](/images/blog/bude-whats-on-guide-october-half-term-2024/1aa1e61a6d23.png)

Crooklets Beach

[Bude’s beaches](https://woodlandsmanorfarm.co.uk/top-holiday-ideas-bude/) are renowned for their beauty and are a must-visit during your stay. The sea is actually at its warmest at this time of year so why not take a dip in the sea or [The Bude Sea Pool](https://www.budeseapool.org/) or even body board, safely of course, always between the flags remember. Autumn is often the best time of the year for surfing too and you can check out our [recommended surf schools here](https://woodlandsmanorfarm.co.uk/local-surf-schools-bude/).

Also, the many beaches are perfect for long walks, beachcombing, crabbing and sandcastle building. Summerleaze Beach, with its tidal pool, is a popular spot where kids can splash around safely. Crooklets Beach and Widemouth Bay are also excellent for a day of seaside fun.

Check out our blog on all the best [Bude beaches here](https://woodlandsmanorfarm.co.uk/woodlands-manor-farm-local-beaches/)

### **Bude Coastal Walks**

![Bude Coastal Walk](/images/blog/bude-whats-on-guide-october-half-term-2024/4458a61e5dcd.jpg)

South West Coastal Path

The [South West Coast Path](https://www.southwestcoastpath.org.uk/) is a must-inclusion in the what’s on guide Bude October Half Term. It runs through Bude, offering some of the most breathtaking coastal walks in the UK. The paths are well-marked and suitable for all ages, providing stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean, dramatic cliffs, and rolling waves. One our our favourites take is from Sandymouth, past Duckpool and ending at The Rectory Tea Rooms or the Bush Inn, for a well deserved rest.

### **Explore Bude Canal**

![Bude Canal ](/images/blog/bude-whats-on-guide-october-half-term-2024/7528a2f58feb.png)

Bude Canal

[The Bude Canal](https://www.facebook.com/BudeCanal/), with its tranquil waters and picturesque surroundings, is perfect for a peaceful stroll or a family bike ride. You can hire bikes locally and follow the canal towpath, enjoying the scenic views and perhaps spotting some local wildlife along the way.

### **Visit Local Attractions**

![The Bude Castle](/images/blog/bude-whats-on-guide-october-half-term-2024/394aebc4abb6.webp)

The Bude Castle

**[Bude Castle](https://www.thecastlebude.co.uk/)**: This historical building houses a museum and art gallery, providing insight into Bude’s rich history and culture. The grounds are lovely for a leisurely walk, and the café offers delicious treats.

They hold an amazing [range of events](https://www.thecastlebude.co.uk/events-at-the-castle-bude/) and exhibitions throughout the year and the Autumn listing can be found here.

**[The Rebel Cinema](https://www.rebelcinema.co.uk/)**: If the weather turns, enjoy a family movie at this charming, independent cinema that showcases the latest releases and classic films.

### **Rainy Day Activities**

Bude is well-prepared for the occasional rainy day, ensuring your family can still have fun indoors.

#### **[Splash Leisure Pool](https://www.better.org.uk/leisure-centre/cornwall/bude-leisure-centre)**

Located in the heart of Bude, the Splash Leisure Pool is perfect for family fun. The pool features a wave machine, water flumes, and a bubble pool, providing hours of entertainment for children and adults alike.

#### **[Indoor Soft Play and Bowling](https://thevenuebude.co.uk/)**

For younger children, **The Venue** offer soft play areas, climbing frames, and slides, as well as bowling. It’s a great way to let the kids burn off some energy while staying dry and warm.

#### **Craft Workshops**

![Welcombe Pottery ](/images/blog/bude-whats-on-guide-october-half-term-2024/5254122cd7e7.png)

Welcombe Pottery

Many local craft shops and galleries offer workshops where you can learn new skills, such as pottery, painting, and jewellery making. It’s a fantastic way to spend a rainy afternoon and come away with a unique souvenir of your trip.

The [Kitchen Front](https://www.thekitchenfront.co.uk/) is a great find for a rainy day. Situated on The Bude Canal, the kids can make some great pottery creations and the parents can enjoy some fabulous tea and cake.

It’s also worth checking out the [arts and craft page on Visit Bude](https://www.visitbude.info/visit/things-to-do/art-craft/) as many of the shops and artists listed hold workshops throughout the year

### **Dining in Bude**

![Lifes a Beach Restaurant Bude](/images/blog/bude-whats-on-guide-october-half-term-2024/a9854551fbe4.webp)

Lifes a Beach Restaurant

A key focus for the whats on guide Bude October Half Term has to be food and dining out, after all, who wants to cook when they are on holiday! Bude boasts a variety of family-friendly eateries, offering everything from traditional Cornish pasties to gourmet meals. Check out our full dining blog on [Bude’s best restaurants here](https://woodlandsmanorfarm.co.uk/local-bude-restaurants/) – quick summary below

#### [**The Beach House**](https://www.beachhousewidemouth.co.uk/)

Located at Widemouth Bay, The Beach House offers stunning sea views and a menu featuring fresh, locally sourced ingredients. The relaxed atmosphere and delicious food make it a perfect spot for a family meal. This really is a hidden gem, and winner of The Times top beach restaurants, so don’t miss it.

#### [**The Olive Tree**](https://www.olivetreebude.co.uk/)

This cozy café is situated by the canal and is known for its friendly service and homemade dishes. It’s an excellent choice for breakfast, lunch, or a coffee break.

#### [**Life’s a Beach**](https://lifesabeach.info/)

For a meal with a view, head to Life’s a Beach, a popular restaurant overlooking Summerleaze Beach. Their menu includes a range of seafood dishes and family favorites, ensuring there’s something for everyone.
