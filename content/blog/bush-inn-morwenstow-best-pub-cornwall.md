## The Bush Inn Ranked #2 in Cornwall's Best Pubs

### A Toast to The Bush Inn, Morwenstow, Bude 

We’re delighted to share that our fantastic local, **[The Bush Inn](https://www.cornishsecrets.co.uk/guide/50-best-pubs-bars-cornwall/)** in Morwenstow, has been named **#2 in the 50 Best Pubs and Bars in Cornwall** by _Cornish Secrets_! Just a short drive from Woodlands Manor Farm, The Bush has long been one of our top recommendations for guests—and now it’s getting the wider recognition it truly deserves.

Warm congratulations to Molly, Toby, and the entire team for creating such a special place, full of charm, hospitality, and community spirit. 🥂

**Why We Love The Bush Inn**

Whether you’re a returning guest or planning your first visit to North Cornwall, [The Bush Inn, Morwenstow](https://www.thebushinnmorwenstow.com/) is a real local highlight. Here’s why it’s such a firm favourite:

-   🍴 **Incredible food** – Local ingredients and hearty dishes with flair
    
-   🍺 **Top-notch drinks** – A well-curated selection of ales, wines, and ciders
    
-   🎶 **Live music** – Regular weekend gigs in a welcoming atmosphere
    
-   🎉 **Bush Fest** – Their own annual festival every June
    
-   🌅 **Sea views** – A perfect place to watch the sunset over the coast
    

It’s everything you could want in a countryside pub, just minutes from your cottage door.

### **Other Local Favourites Featured**

The Bush Inn Morwenstow isn’t the only local hero on the list. We were also pleased to see several other Bude favourites mentioned:

-   [**The Preston Gate Inn**](https://prestongateinn.co.uk/) – A traditional pub with great food and friendly vibes
    
-   [**The Barrel**](https://www.facebook.com/thebarrelatbude/?locale=en_GB) – Bude’s original micropub, loved for its unique beers
    
-   [**North Coast Wine Bar**](https://www.northcoastwinelounge.co.uk/) – A sophisticated spot for wine lovers
    

We’re lucky to be surrounded by such a brilliant food and drink scene—yet another reason why our corner of Cornwall is such a special place to stay.
