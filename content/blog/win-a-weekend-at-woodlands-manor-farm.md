## Win A Weekend Away at Woodlands

To celebrate five years of running Woodlands Manor Farm, we’re running a social competition and giving a lucky winner a chance to enjoy a weekend in one of our cozy cottages or luxurious glamping yurts.

The lucky winner and guests will be able to stay in one of the following Cottages or Yurts.

**Choose From:**

[The Stables](https://woodlandsmanorfarm.co.uk/the-stables/) (Sleeps 4)  
[Lavender](https://woodlandsmanorfarm.co.uk/lavender-cottage/) (Sleeps 4)  
[Coach House](https://woodlandsmanorfarm.co.uk/the-coach-house/) (Sleeps 2)  
[Honeysuckle](https://woodlandsmanorfarm.co.uk/honeysuckle-cottage/) (Sleeps 2)  
[Fenton Yurt](https://woodlandsmanorfarm.co.uk/fenton-yurt/) (Sleeps 4)  
[Budhyn Yurt](https://woodlandsmanorfarm.co.uk/budhyn-yurt/) (Sleeps 4)

The winner can book for up to 4 people and up to 3 nights. The booking will need to be booked to take place from **February 2025 to May 2025** and is subject to availability in the listed cottages only.

Of course the winners will benefit from all of the farm facilities, such as the indoor swimming pool, games-room, playing field, woodlands and the feed the animals experience, as well as the local beaches and the many activities and restaurants that Bude has to offer.
