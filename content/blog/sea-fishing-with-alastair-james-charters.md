## Sea Fishing with Alastair James Charters

If you’re staying at **Woodlands Manor Farm** and want to experience the best of **sea fishing in North Devon and Cornwall**, get in touch with **Alastair James Charters**. Whether you’re a seasoned sea fisher or new to the sport, Alastair James offers a variety of sea fishing trips. Trips that let you explore the stunning coastline while enjoying some of the best fishing spots in the area.

### **Cornwall Sea Fishing Trips** 

If you’re passionate about sea fishing, then you’ll love the trips Alastair James Charters offers. Departing from the historic Clovelly Harbour, you’ll head out on the fast and well-equipped Blue Fin to some of the best sea fishing locations around the North Devon Coast. These waters are packed with species like bass, mackerel, and pollock, giving you plenty of chances to reel in a big catch.

For a more hands-on experience, try the specialised guided fishing trips. These focus on sea fishing techniques for targeting specific species. Whether you’re an experienced fisherman or just getting started, the crew will guide you through the best fishing methods and show you the top spots.

### Coastal and Lundy Island Sightseeing Trips

If you’re looking for a more relaxed experience, Alastair James Charters offers fantastic sightseeing trips along the rugged and unspoiled North Devon coastline. These trips are a perfect way to enjoy the stunning scenery as the sun dips below the horizon. As you cruise along the coast, you’ll be treated to spectacular views of dramatic cliffs, tranquil beaches, and sparkling waters. At the same time, you will witness the beautiful colours of the sky at sunset. It’s the perfect way to unwind and take in the natural beauty of Cornwall in a truly unique way.

For those seeking adventure and serenity, take a trip to Lundy Island. This remote island, surrounded by crystal-clear waters, is a hidden gem. The island is home to an abundance of wildlife, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers and those seeking peace and tranquillity. The experienced crew will ensure a safe and comfortable journey, allowing you to explore the beauty of Lundy Island at your leisure.

### Champagne Sightseeing Trips

For an even more indulgent experience, why not treat yourself to a champagne sightseeing trip? Relax and enjoy the gorgeous views of the coastline while sipping champagne on board the ‘Bluefin’. Whether it’s a special occasion or just a way to make your Cornwall visit even more memorable, this trip offers the perfect blend of luxury and scenic beauty.

### **Why Choose Alastair James Charters for Sea Fishing?**

Alastair James Charters is your go-to choice for sea fishing in North Devon. The experienced crew, headed by skipper Jim Dickson, knows the waters inside and out.  Ensuring you have a safe, enjoyable, and successful fishing experience, whatever your age or experience level.

Jim Dickson is the fully qualified skipper, keen fisherman, and local gamekeeper at the heart of Alastair James Charters.

Jim is a born and bred local who has been in and out of the Clovelly shoreline for over 30 years. Making him the perfect guide to some of North Devon’s best-kept secrets and Fishing grounds.

With the Blue Fin being fast and fully equipped, you can rest assured that you’ll not only have the best crew but also be comfortable while you focus on making that big catch.
