All cottages & yurts • Dog-friendly

Dogs welcome

We love dogs at Woodlands Manor Farm — this is Bailey, our Bernedoodle. All well-behaved dogs are welcome at every property. To make sure everyone has the best holiday — dogs included — please read and follow the guidelines below. They are there for everyone’s safety, especially the dogs.

🐕 Please tell us you’re bringing a dog when you book

Let us know your dog’s breed, age and temperament. An additional deposit of £25 per dog is required. Maximum two dogs per cottage.

🔗 On a lead around the farm AT ALL TIMES

We have free-range chickens, farm animals and neighbouring livestock. Dogs must be on a lead on the drive and around the cottages at all times, without exception.

🌿 Free to run in the meadow & woods

Dogs can run free in the meadow and bluebell woodland — a wonderful space for a good explore. Please be careful near sheep and cows in adjacent fields.

🛌 Dogs not allowed upstairs or in bedrooms

For the comfort of all guests, dogs should not be allowed upstairs or in the bedrooms in any of the cottages.

💊 Sofa cover required for sofa access

If you want to let your dog on the sofa, please bring your own protective cover. Additional cleaning charges may apply if sofas are left dirty after a dog stay.

💰 Do not leave dogs unattended

Dogs should not be left alone in the cottage or yurt unless they are in a crate and you are confident they will not cause damage or disturbance to other guests.

✓ Come prepared — bring everything they need

Please bring your dog’s bed, towels, food & water bowls, blankets and crate if needed. If you forget anything, let us know and we will help where we can.

Dog-friendly amenities & services

💧
Outdoor tap

An outdoor tap in the main courtyard for cleaning muddy paws before going back into the cottage. Use it after the woodland walk — it will be needed.

🐕
Dog sitting & walks

If you need to leave your dog for a longer period, please talk to us. On-site paid dog sitting and dog walking services can be arranged. We want your dog to be happy all day.

🏠
Doggy day care — Wags

Wags Doggy Day Care is available locally if you need a full day out without your dog. Find Wags on Facebook.

✂️
Dog grooming in Bude

Two excellent dog groomers in Bude: Lilly’s Dog Grooming and Barkers of Bude.

🚻
Vet in Bude

If you need veterinary assistance during your stay, we are happy to help. The vet we use is Tamar Vets — Bude branch.

Dog beach policy — seasonal restrictions

✓ Dog-friendly ALL year

  • Duckpool Beach (National Trust) — 2 miles
  • Northcott Mouth (National Trust) — 4 miles
  • Sandymouth Beach — dogs must be under control in summer
  • Black Rock Beach (south of Widemouth Bay)
  • Millook Beach

⚠️ Seasonal restrictions (15 May – 30 Sep, 10am–6pm)

  • Crooklets Beach — no dogs 15 May to 30 Sep, 10am–6pm
  • Summerleaze Beach — dogs on leads 21 May to 30 Sep, 10am–6pm
  • Widemouth Bay (main beach) — no dogs 15 May to 30 Sep, 10am–6pm
