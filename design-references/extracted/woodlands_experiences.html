Weddings &
celebrations

Woodlands is the perfect location for an intimate wedding or special celebration. With the ability to sleep over 40 guests on the farm, everyone can relax and enjoy the day in perfect Cornish comfort, admiring the picturesque valley setting.

  • Sleeps 40+ across 9 properties — exclusive use available
  • Tipis, marquees and caterers can be arranged
  • Intimate valley setting, 2 miles from the Cornish coast
  • Heated indoor pool and full farm facilities for guests
  • Ruth and Andy will work with you from first enquiry to the day
