Places to eat in Bude

Bude and the surrounding area has so many fantastic places to eat, you are genuinely spoilt for choice — from fine dining and fresh seafood to the best beach cafes and what might be one of the finest bakeries in the UK. Every cottage has a fully equipped kitchen, but on those days when you don’t feel like cooking, here are our top picks.

On or near the seafront

Life’s a Beach
Seafood • Bistro • 6 miles

Overlooking Summerleaze Beach, this cafe and bistro is the perfect place for a light bite or a sit-down meal. Freshly sourced seafood is their speciality — but something for everyone.

Visit website →
Elements
Fine Dining • Seafront • 6 miles

A stylish restaurant at The Beach hotel, overlooking Summerleaze. Fresh fish, steak, pizza and pasta — a varied menu with a stunning view. One of Bude’s best evening out options.

Visit website →
The Deck
British • Global • 6 miles

British cuisine with a global influence. Their outside decking area is the perfect place to watch the sun go down over Bude. The menu draws on the overseas adventures of the owners.

Visit website →
Rosie’s Kitchen
Beach Cafe • Crooklets • 6 miles

A bustling beachside cafe at Crooklets Beach with an enclosed sundeck overlooking the Atlantic. Positively bursting with atmosphere and Cornish charm. A Bude institution.

Visit website →
The Beach House
Beach Bar • Widemouth Bay • 8 miles

Home of The Break Beach Bar — listed in the Times’ Top 20 beach-side bars & restaurants in the UK. Private access to Widemouth Bay. One of the best locations in Cornwall, full stop.

Visit website →
Crooklets Beach Cafe
Cafe • Crooklets • 6 miles

A family-run cafe at the edge of Crooklets Beach using the freshest local ingredients. Daily specials, hearty burgers, sandwiches, hot drinks and a range of delicious cakes.

Visit website →

In Bude town

The Bank
Tapas • Bude • 6 miles

Traditional Spanish tapas combined with fresh Cornish produce. A truly unique dining experience. Whether a romantic meal for two or a large group, The Bank never disappoints.

Visit website →
The Barge
Canal Barge • Bude • 6 miles

A repurposed canal barge on the Bude Canal. Locally sourced British dishes in spectacularly memorable surroundings. Tea and cake or a full meal — both brilliant.

Learn more →
Olive Tree
Mediterranean • Canal-side • 6 miles

Next to the Bude Canal with Mediterranean influences throughout the menu. Dine outside in fine weather. The vegan fish & chips is a crowd-pleaser worth trying even if you’re not vegan.

Visit website →
Bocca Pizza
Neapolitan Pizza • Bude • 6 miles

Classic Neapolitan pizzas, calzone and antipasti. Mainly take-away but worth every minute of the short ride to Bude. Gluten free and vegan options available.

Visit website →
The Electric Bakery
Bakery • Bude • 6 miles

Not just a bakery — a mighty fine Electric Bakery. The bread, cakes, sandwiches and lunches are simply heavenly. You can also book a sourdough course with master baker Ben.

Visit website →
The Preston Gate
Pub • Bude • 6 miles

A popular Bude inn serving great traditional pub food — Sunday roasts, fish and chips and steak. So popular with locals and visitors that booking in advance is strongly recommended.

Visit website →

Worth the drive

The Bush Inn
13th C. Pub • Morwenstow • 4 miles

One of Cornwall’s oldest pubs, with a history dating back to around 950AD. Rich in character, fabulous pub food — and if you mention you’re from Woodlands Manor Farm, Andy is a local and you’ll be guaranteed a warm welcome.

Visit website →
The Farmers Arms
Michelin Guide • Woolfardisworthy • 11 miles

A stunning countryside pub using meat and produce from their own farm. Traditional pub grub with a contemporary touch — included in the 2022 Michelin Guide. Definitely worth the journey.

Visit website →
The Old Smithy Inn
13th C. Thatched Inn • Welcombe • 8 miles

A 13th-century thatched inn at the top of the valley in Welcombe. Fresh food cooked on the premises with a strong bias towards local and British ingredients. A real hidden gem.

Visit website →
The Rectory Tearooms
Cream Teas • Morwenstow • 4 miles

If you like cream teas and Cornish pasties, you cannot miss these tearooms. Too many awards to mention — including a feature on the BBC’s Hairy Bikers. An unmissable Cornish experience.

Visit website →
Pattard
Fine Dining • Hartland • 11 miles

Modern British and French fine dining in a gorgeous barn conversion near Hartland. Locally sourced seasonal ingredients and glowing reviews since opening in 2020. A special occasion destination.

Visit website →
Rick Stein
Seafood • Padstow • 50 miles

Rick Stein’s flagship seafood restaurant in the heart of Padstow. Over an hour from Woodlands — but if you are looking for something truly special, it is worth every mile. Lobster thermidor, turbot hollandaise, Indonesian seafood curry.
