The little things that matter

The little extras

At Woodlands Manor Farm we provide all the little things that make your holiday truly special. At every turn, we aim to make your stay as relaxing and comfortable as possible — so you can focus on the important stuff: time with the people you love.

👤
Babies & toddlers

We are extremely baby and child-friendly. We can provide: cot, highchair, baby bath, nappy bin, changing mat, bottle steriliser, plastic cutlery, bowls, plates and socket covers. For toddlers: bed guard, potty, step, toilet seat and booster seat. Just ask when booking.

🧚
Bed linen & towels

All properties include bed linen and towels as standard. A change of linen is supplied for stays of two weeks or more. A change of towels is provided each week. Please bring your own cot bedding and beach towels.

📶
Super-fast broadband

Woodlands Manor Farm has a super-fast broadband connection and complimentary WiFi throughout all cottages and yurts. Strong enough for working, streaming and video calls simultaneously.

🛒
Grocery delivery

Tesco, Waitrose, Asda and Sainsbury’s all deliver directly to the farm. Our postcode is EX23 9HT. Ordering before you arrive is a popular option — everything waiting for you on the doorstep.

🍳
Private chef — Nicky

If you would like a lovely meal ready for your arrival, or have a special occasion and would rather leave it to an expert — Nicky our private chef can deliver to your cottage. Contact us for the current menu.

💅
In-cottage spa — Leanne

Why not treat yourself to a bit of pampering? Leanne is a qualified therapist offering a wide range of beauty and massage treatments, carried out in the comfort of your own cottage. Contact us for the current menu.

🌞
Art experiences

Bude has a vibrant art community. Local artists offer courses from watercolour seascapes with Hannah Jenkinson to pottery at Welcombe Pottery or wood turning at Hide Studio. Contact us to arrange.

♻️
Recycling on site

We have recycling bins on site for glass, paper, cardboard and tin. We provide kitchen essentials (salt, pepper, herbs) to cut down on waste and use eco-friendly cleaning products throughout.

⚡
EV charging

An EV charging point is available on site for guests. Free to use during your stay.

Any special requests? Just ask.

Andy and Ruth are always happy to help make your stay extra special.
