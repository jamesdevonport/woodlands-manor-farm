Everything you need

Glamping without compromise

Each yurt is 5.8 metres wide and 3 metres high at the centre — proper space to breathe. We’ve thought of everything so you can simply arrive and unwind, from crisp white linen to USB charging points and your own kitchen cupboard stocked with the essentials.

  • 🛌Super king (6ft) double bed
  • 🏡Two single beds
  • 🔥Nordpeis Orion wood-burner
  • 🛀Private shower room with WC
  • 🍳Two-ring induction hob
  • 🥥Microwave & fridge/ice box
  • ☕Kettle, toaster, cafetière
  • 🥺Full crockery & cutlery
  • 📴Outdoor picnic table
  • 🔥BBQ & fire pit
  • 📶Free super-fast WiFi
  • 🔋USB charging points
  • 💁Crisp white linen & towels
  • 🐕Dogs very welcome
