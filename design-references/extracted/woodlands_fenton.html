Glamping in Cornwall’s finest valley

Proper luxury,
under canvas

Fenton — meaning 'spring' or 'stream' in Cornish — captures the essence of the valley it sits in: fresh, alive, and quietly beautiful. Positioned alongside Budhyn in the meadow at Woodlands Manor Farm, Fenton is fully equipped to the same high specification — Nordpeis stove, super king bed, private kitchen, private shower room and a Weber BBQ under cover outside.

Wake up to birdsong and valley views, then spend your days exactly as you choose — whether that's in the pool, on the beach, feeding the animals with Ruth, or simply sitting by the fire pit as the evening draws in. Woodlands at its best.

Everything you need

What’s included

🔥
Nordpeis Orion stove

A premium high-end wood-burning stove at the heart of the yurt — keeps you toasty warm on cool evenings and creates an atmosphere you can’t replicate anywhere else.

🍲
Weber BBQ & fire pit

A full Weber BBQ with a large covered outdoor area — so you can grill come rain or shine. A fire pit completes the evening. Firewood provided on arrival.

🍳
Private kitchen

Fridge/ice box, microwave, toaster, electric kettle, two-ring induction hob, folding table and chairs, plus everything you need to cook and eat — pans, utensils, crockery, cutlery, glasses.

🛁
Private shower room

Your own dedicated shower room with WC, hand basin and a large heated towel rail — warm and private, just steps from your yurt door.

🛌
Super king + two singles

A luxurious super king-size (6ft) double bed made up with quality bed linen, plus two single beds — perfect for a couple with two children.

🏈
Indoor pool access

Full access to the large heated indoor swimming pool, maintained at 30°C year-round, open 8am to 8pm every day. Included with your stay, no booking required.

🎮
Games room

Table tennis, pool table, foosball, giant Connect Four, air hockey and a full soft play area — all included with your yurt booking.

🐄
Feed the animals

Sunday and Wednesday mornings, Ruth takes guests to feed the pigs, goats, alpacas, ponies, rabbits and chickens. A highlight guests mention in every review.
