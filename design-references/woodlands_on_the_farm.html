Included with every stay

Everything on the farm is yours to enjoy

Once you arrive at Woodlands you can relax and recharge. Every facility on this page is included with your stay — no booking, no extra charge, just turn up and enjoy.

🏈
Heated Indoor Pool

Maintained at a warm 30°C year-round. Lane swimming or kids playing — there are no limits to the fun. Open 8am to 8pm daily. No lifeguard — children must be supervised at all times.

🎮
Games Room

Soft play, table tennis, foosball, giant Connect Four, darts and pool. A hot favourite with guests of all ages — great for rainy days or just because.

🏧
Playing Field

A large flat field with 5-a-side football goals, a proper playground with swings, slide and climbing frame, and enough sky to fly a kite all afternoon.

🌿
Bluebell Woodland

Simply magical in April and May when the floor is carpeted in bluebells. Dogs welcome but bring wellies — it can get muddy by the stream in winter.

🐄
Feed the Animals

Every Sunday and Wednesday morning Ruth takes guests to feed the pigs, sheep, alpacas, horses, goats, rabbits and chickens. One of the most-loved moments of any Woodlands stay.

🌎
Local Beaches & Walks

Duckpool Beach is 2 miles away. Bude and Summerleaze are 6 miles. The South West Coast Path is just 2 miles from the farm gate. Ask Andy and Ruth for their favourite walks.

Pool details

30°C year-round — whatever the weather

Our heated indoor pool is consistently the thing guests mention most in their reviews. It is a proper family swimming pool — not a plunge pool — maintained at 30°C and open every single day of the year from 8am to 8pm.

  • Temperature: 30°C maintained year-round
  • Open: 8am – 8pm daily (including Bank Holidays)
  • Free to all cottage and yurt guests — no booking needed
  • No lifeguard — children under 16 must be supervised by an adult at all times
  • Shower facilities available adjacent to the pool building
